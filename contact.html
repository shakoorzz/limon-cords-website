Frequently Asked Questions

What custom lengths are available?

We can manufacture bungee cords in any length from 6 inches to 20 feet. Standard lengths (12", 17", 24") are available for immediate shipping.

How long does custom manufacturing take?

Standard custom orders take 1-2 weeks. Rush orders can be completed in 1-3 days for an additional fee. Planning ahead helps ensure best pricing and timing.

What's the minimum order quantity?

For standard products, there's no minimum. Custom colors require a minimum of 50 pieces. Bulk orders start at 1000 feet with special pricing.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes! Volume pricing is available for orders over 100 pieces, with additional discounts for orders over 500 and 1000+ pieces.

Can you match specific color requirements?

We can match most Pantone colors and provide color samples for approval before production. Custom color orders require minimum quantities.

What weight capacities are available?

Our standard range includes 200 lb, 261 lb, and 555 lb capacity cords. Higher capacities available for special applications.
